Our mission is to make ownership of real estate radically accessible, cost-effective and simple. The idea of this website project started few months ago when the COVID-19 started to emerge into pandemical situation. We analyzed the situation on real estate market in Bosnia and Herzegovina, and figured out that there are no options left when it comes to property buying. The whole society has come to an unique situation of having a lot of spare time for research and looking for options when it comes to our place of living. Our goal is to unite anyone who wants to sell or rent any real estate property and present all ofers in one place. All of our information regarding specific offers are taken from the actual real estate services in Sarajevo, and we have no intentions of misusing any information we retrieved from these services. =======
